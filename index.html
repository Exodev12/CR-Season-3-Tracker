✦   Setting Up Auto-Sync (JSONBin)
Step 1 — Go to jsonbin.io and create a free account.
Step 2 — Click your avatar (top right) → API Keys → copy your Master Key (starts with $2a$ or $2b$).
Step 3 — In this tracker, click ☁ Sync in the header. Paste your Master Key. Click ✦ Create Bin for Me.
Step 4 — The tracker creates a private bin and shows its ID. Copy that Bin ID.
Step 5 — Done. Progress now syncs automatically on every change.

On another device — open the same tracker URL, click ☁ Sync, paste the same Master Key and Bin ID, click Save & Connect. Your progress loads immediately.
